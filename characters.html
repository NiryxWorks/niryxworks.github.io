The Nekos of Nekophoria

Meet the sisters that bring warmth, laughter, and charm to the neko-café experience.

Kurema

“Everything will be okay if you smile, nya~!”

Adorable, dreamy, and naive. The spark of coffee and the source of Nekophoria's tenderness. Although she's the tallest, she's the most innocent of the three.

Behind her sweetness lies a wounded heart that has learned to trust with hope. She believes in the little things and collects spoons because she thinks they're "princess-like."

Bomi

“I’m not cute! ... And don’t touch my head!”

Responsible, organized, and strong-willed. She cooks, organizes, and takes care of her sisters as if the world depended on it.

She had to grow up quickly, sacrificing her childhood to protect others. Although she appears strict, she secretly records what makes her happy in a diary she doesn't want anyone to see.

Kuramiru

“Did you know that cold coffee can have more personality than hot coffee?”

The older sister, carefree and observant. The barista and occasional guitarist. She accompanies without imposing, guiding from the shadows.

She transformed pain into humor and freedom. She has secret recordings where she sings songs with funny lyrics about her sisters.
