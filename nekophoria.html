Big news!

19/4/2025

I'm working on a new major project: Nekophoria (Neko-Café) —a game about catgirls running a cozy café alongside you, the player.

It's being developed side by side with Colorful Hearts (Project: Blue Skies), another story-rich title full of emotion, mystery, and choices.

Both games are crafted with love, passion, and an obsession for quality. Every pixel, every line of code, every moment —they're all built to make sure you feel the effort behind them.

I won’t set any release dates (because deadlines are evil 😾), but if all goes well, both games should be ready by mid to late 2026.

Thanks for following my journey —much love and neko kisses! 🐾💌
