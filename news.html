Niryx Dev Update – Closing Cycles and New Paths

Hello everyone. This is a short message from the creative heart of Niryx Works.

Sometimes ideas come like a beautiful storm... but not all of them are ready to become reality. That's why today I want to tell you that two projects will be put on indefinite hiatus:

  • Love and Music — a project full of sensitivity and potential, which may resurface in the future.
  • I remember you last summer / Colorful Hearts — a story that began as a tribute, but today I feel that it does not represent what I want to tell with Niryx.

Instead, I'm going to focus all my energy on the project that best represents my vision: Nekophoria, and another.

This isn't a goodbye forever, it's just a reconfiguration.

Thank you for being there and believing in what I do.

– Matías | Niryx Works
